About

I am a Software Engineer (Backend) at Roshan Company in London, where I build scalable backend systems for AI-powered applications, focusing on performance, modularity, and maintainability. I design and optimize REST APIs and data pipelines, integrate machine learning models, and maintain production services on Linux servers.

Previously, I was an AI Researcher at the Computational Intelligence Laboratory, developing machine learning models for cancer and COVID-19 detection from medical imaging and deep neural networks for imagined speech classification using EEG signals. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Noshirvani University of Technology and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence at Queen Mary University of London.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and learning languages.
